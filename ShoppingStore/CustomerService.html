FAQs



Q: How do I choose the right shoe size?

A: We recommend using our size chart as a reference to find the perfect fit for your feet. You can find the size chart on our website under the "Size Guide" section.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: We offer a 30-day return policy for unused and unworn sneakers. Please refer to our "Returns and Exchanges" page for detailed instructions on how to initiate a return.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?

A: Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. During the checkout process, you can enter your shipping address to check if we deliver to your location.

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: Shipping times may vary depending on your location. We typically process and ship orders within 1-3 business days. Once shipped, domestic orders usually arrive within 3-7 business days, while international orders may take longer.

Q: Can I track my order?

A: Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your shipment on our website or the courier's tracking system.

Q: Do you offer free returns?

A: Yes, we provide free returns for orders within the domestic region. Please check our "Returns and Exchanges" page for detailed information on our return policy and instructions on how to initiate a return.

Q: Are your sneakers suitable for wide feet?

A: Yes, we offer a range of sneakers in different widths to accommodate various foot sizes. Please refer to our size chart and product descriptions for information on the available widths for each model.

Q: Can I cancel or change my order after it's placed?

A: We strive to process orders quickly, but if you need to make changes or cancel your order, please contact our customer support as soon as possible. We'll do our best to assist you, but please note that once an order is shipped, it cannot be canceled or modified.

Q: Are your sneakers suitable for sports activities?

A: Yes, many of our sneakers are designed for sports activities. They offer features like enhanced cushioning, stability, and traction to support athletic performance. Look for models specifically labeled as "sports" or "performance" sneakers.

Q: Do you offer warranty on your sneakers?

A: Yes, we provide a limited warranty on manufacturing defects. If you believe your sneakers have a defect, please reach out to our customer support with your order details and a description of the issue. We'll be happy to assist you with the warranty process.
